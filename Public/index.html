FAQ

Where is the Post-UTME exam center?
Most exams hold at the Main Auditorium or ICT centers. Check your slip for details. Arrive at least 30 minutes early to locate your specific exam hall.
Can I come with my phone?
Phones are usually not allowed inside the exam hall. Keep them safe outside or leave them at your accommodation to avoid distractions or confiscation.
How do I find my faculty?
Use our map above to navigate easily to your faculty or hostel. You can also ask for directions at the information desk near the main gate.
What documents do I need?
Bring your printed exam slip, valid ID (school ID, national ID, or passport), and writing materials. It's also advisable to bring a water bottle.
